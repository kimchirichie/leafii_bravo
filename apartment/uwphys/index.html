Alcohol – enhances the production of more stomach acid.

Caffeine – increases the amount of stomach acid and the concentration level.

Smoking - increases the acidity of the small intestine and produces an environment for H. Pylori infection. Smoking constricts the small blood vessels in the stomach reducing blood flow. Prolonged behavior will lead to damage of stomach.

Zollinger -Ellison syndrome – is a rare condition in which one or more tumors form in your pancreas or the first part of the small intestine. This results to your stomach producing of more stomach acid. Zollinger or Ellison syndrome may result in acid reflux and heartburn.

Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD, Acid Reflux) – due to poor closure of the lower esophageal sphincter stomach contents come back up into the esophagus resulting in either symptoms or complications. Simply put, the mucus lining as the stomach does not to protect you from stomach acid.

Ulcerative colitis - is an inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).it result in prolonged inflammation in your digestive tract and affects the inner lining of your large intestine (colon).
